About Me

Hello, I’m Justin Corley, a graduate student specializing in Data Science & Analytics at Old Dominion University, where I also obtained my B.S. in Computer Science. I have a strong passion for leveraging programming, data analysis, and emerging technologies to develop solutions that enhance innovation, streamline processes, and improve clarity within complex systems.

Before I transitioned into the tech field, I served as a Medical Laboratory Technician in the U.S. Air Force. During that time, I gained invaluable insights into the importance of precision, process optimization, and collaboration in high-pressure environments. Following my service, I joined CareDx, a biotechnology company focused on transplant diagnostics, where I contributed to clinical research by processing biological samples and quantifying genetic data. This experience deepened my appreciation for how technology and data can significantly enhance human outcomes.

Since then, I have continued to embrace that same mindset in my roles as a developer, researcher, and project lead. Whether I’m developing web applications like WiseTraveler or managing the operations of a small business, I am dedicated to transforming complexity into clarity, converting data into meaningful insights, and turning innovative ideas into impactful solutions.

In my freetime I enjoy spending time with my fiancé and our dog, Zeus, gaming, golfing, snowboarding, or working on my car!
